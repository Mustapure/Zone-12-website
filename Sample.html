Built for Leaders. Driven by Purpose.

Junior Chamber International is a global network of changemakers building better leaders for a better world. For over 100 years, we’ve helped young people grow through real-world projects, leadership training, and meaningful connections that drive positive change.

Four Areas of Opportunity

JCI develops young leaders by empowering personal growth, creating community impact, fostering international cooperation, and supporting business and entrepreneurship. Through these four areas, we shape active citizens who drive positive change locally and globally.

Fuel Global Impact Through Strategic Collaboration

At JCI, a global youth leadership organization, we believe in the power of partnership to create sustainable change. By working with companies, institutions, and organizations that share our values, we’re building stronger communities and developing leaders for a changing world.

Together with our partners, we support initiatives that develop young people, drive innovation, and address global challenges—from grassroots projects to international campaigns. Whether through sponsorship, co-branded programs, or long-term alliances, every partnership helps us scale our mission across borders.
