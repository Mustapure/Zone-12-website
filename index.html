Our Foundation

Creed, Vision & Mission

The JCI Creed:
"We believe: That faith in God gives meaning and purpose to human life; That the brotherhood of man transcends the sovereignty of nations; That economic justice can best be won by free men through free enterprise; That government should be of laws rather than of men; That earth's great treasure lies in human personality; And that service to humanity is the best work of life."
Meet Our Leaders

To be the foremost global network of young leaders creating positive change in communities and beyond. We envision a world where every young person has the opportunity to grow, lead, and make a lasting difference in their society.

To provide leadership development opportunities that empower young people to create sustainable impact in society. We fulfill this through business entrepreneurship, international cooperation, individual growth, and community action.

Why Join Us

Why Join JCI India?

Joining JCI (Junior Chamber International) India offers a multitude of benefits, including leadership development, global networking, skill-building, community impact, and exciting opportunities. It provides a platform for young people to grow, lead, and create positive change in their communities and around the world.

Leadership Growth

Develop real-world leadership skills through hands-on training and mentorship programmes.

Global Network

Connect with over 200,000 JCI members across 120 nations and build a powerful global network.

Community Impact

Drive meaningful change in your local community through impactful projects and initiatives.

Skill Building

Enhance your professional skills through workshops, training sessions, and real-world challenges.

Recognition

Earn prestigious awards and certifications that are recognised by global organisations and employers.

Business Opportunities

Access exclusive business networking events and entrepreneurship development platforms.
